Ingredients:
 - 2 cups granulated sugar
 - 1 cup milk
 - 2 squares of baking chocolate
 - 1 tablespoon butter
 - 2 tablespoons marshmallow
 - 3/4 cup of crushed nuts

Directions:
 1. Combine all ingredients other than marshmallow and nuts in medium sized saucepan.
 2. Boil approximately 20 minutes or until soft ball forms when fudge dropped in cold water
 3. Remove from stove
 4. Beat 1 minute by hand
 5. Add marshmallow and nuts
 6. Beat until blended
 7. Pour into shallow pan to cool and harden
